WOMEN IN THE TECH INDUSTRY Date: 09/08/2024
By: Amber Jones

In honour of Women's Day, we have curated a special piece celebrating and analysing women within the tech industry.

STATISTICS OF WOMEN IN THE TECH INDUSTRY:

According to an article from Women in tech ZA, an amount of 23% out of a total of 236 000 roles in ICT are occupied by women.
Though that is a downside, we can look up to an admire the perseverance of women: as another article, published by Women Tech network, states that there has been a great improvement of the number of women making up the tech industry.
The statistics state that there have been at least a 26% increase from the 2000s up until now.

SUCCESSFUL WOMEN IN THE TECH INDUSTRY:

There are many successful women in different fields and areas of tech. We will however be looking at a few of the most successful women in tech in South Africa.
According to an article released by Heels & Tech; they make mention of Zandile Keebine who is the co-founder of GirlCode. Founder of Girlhype: Baratang Miya. And Mmaki Jantjies who was one of the first black female South Africans to obtain a PhD in computer science whilst she was young.
